Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which refunds may be granted for purchases made through Kathika. By purchasing any digital product, subscription, or service on Kathika, you agree to this Refund Policy.

2. Digital Products

Most purchases on Kathika involve digital products and services, including Mohars, Premium Story Access, Superfan Memberships, and other platform features. Due to the nature of digital goods, purchases are generally considered final once access has been granted.

3. Mohar Purchases

Mohars purchased within Kathika are digital platform credits. Once Mohars are successfully credited to a user account, they are generally non-refundable and non-transferable.

4. Premium Story Access

When Mohars or other payment methods are used to unlock premium content, the transaction is normally considered complete after access is granted. Refunds are generally not available for consumed digital content.

5. Superfan Memberships

Superfan subscriptions provide access to creator-specific benefits. Membership fees are generally non-refundable once the subscription becomes active, except where required by applicable law.

6. Subscription Services

Recurring subscriptions may automatically renew according to the selected plan. Users may cancel future renewals through the platform or through the payment provider used during purchase.

7. Eligible Refund Cases

Refund requests may be considered in situations such as:

  • Duplicate payments
  • Technical billing errors
  • Unauthorized transactions verified through investigation
  • Failure to receive purchased benefits due to technical issues

8. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will generally not be provided for:

  • Completed digital content purchases
  • Used Mohars
  • Consumed subscription benefits
  • Change of personal preference
  • Failure to read product descriptions before purchase

9. Refund Review Process

Each refund request is reviewed individually. Kathika reserves the right to request supporting information necessary to verify claims and investigate payment disputes.

10. Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically processed within a reasonable period. Actual timelines may vary depending on the payment provider, banking institution, or platform used to complete the transaction.

11. Fraud Prevention

Kathika actively monitors suspicious refund activity. Refund abuse, chargeback fraud, or repeated misuse of payment systems may result in account restrictions or permanent suspension.

12. Third-Party Payment Providers

Payments may be processed through third-party services such as Google Play Billing, Razorpay, or other authorized providers. Additional refund conditions imposed by those providers may also apply.

13. Google Play Purchases

For purchases completed through Google Play, users may also be subject to Google Play's refund policies and procedures. Requests may need to be submitted directly through Google where applicable.

14. Policy Updates

Kathika may update this Refund Policy periodically. Updated versions will be published on this page along with the revised effective date.
