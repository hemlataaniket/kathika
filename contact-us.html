Get in Touch

We value our readers, writers, creators, and community members. If you have questions, concerns, feedback, or require assistance regarding any Kathika service, please contact us using the information below.

Support Services

Our support team can assist with:

  • Account-related issues
  • Login and access problems
  • Story publishing assistance
  • Payment and subscription concerns
  • Superfan membership issues
  • Technical support requests
  • Content moderation appeals
  • Privacy and data requests
  • Copyright complaints
  • Bug reports and feature feedback

Support Email

Response Time

We aim to respond to most support requests within 48 business hours. Response times may vary during holidays, maintenance periods, or high-volume support periods.

Copyright Complaints

If you believe content on Kathika infringes your intellectual property rights, please include:

  • Your full name
  • Contact information
  • Description of copyrighted work
  • Location of the allegedly infringing content
  • Supporting documentation

Privacy Requests

For privacy-related requests, account deletion requests, or data access inquiries, please contact us using the support email listed above.

Feedback & Suggestions

We welcome feedback from our community. Suggestions for new features, improvements, accessibility enhancements, and user experience upgrades are always appreciated.

Platform Information

Kathika is a storytelling platform designed to connect readers and writers through creative content, interactive communities, and creator support features.
