Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kathika, a storytelling platform that enables users to read, write, publish, share, and support literary content. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Kathika collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and profile details
  • Email address
  • Profile picture and cover images
  • Stories, drafts, chapters, comments, and reviews
  • Posts published in Pravah
  • Messages and communication data
  • Superfan subscription information
  • Payment and transaction records
  • Device information and analytics

3. Account Information

When you register for Kathika, we collect information necessary to create and manage your account. This information helps us authenticate users, secure accounts, and provide personalized services.

4. User Generated Content

Kathika allows users to publish stories, comments, reviews, and posts. Content published on the platform may be visible to other users according to the visibility settings selected by the creator.

5. Story Ownership

Authors retain ownership of their original content. By publishing content on Kathika, authors grant Kathika a limited license to display, distribute, and promote content within the platform.

6. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve services
  • To manage user accounts
  • To personalize recommendations
  • To process payments and subscriptions
  • To detect abuse and fraud
  • To enforce community guidelines
  • To deliver notifications
  • To maintain platform security

7. Superfan Services

Kathika provides optional Superfan features that allow readers to support authors through subscriptions and other engagement features. Information related to memberships and support activity may be processed to provide these services.

8. Payments and Transactions

Kathika may offer paid services, subscriptions, Mohar purchases, and author support systems. Payment processing is handled through authorized third-party payment providers. Kathika does not store complete card or banking credentials.

9. Content Moderation

To maintain a safe and welcoming community, Kathika may review content using automated systems and human moderation processes. Content violating platform rules may be removed without prior notice.

10. Analytics and Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage information such as reading activity, feature usage, crash reports, search activity, and engagement metrics to improve the platform experience.

11. Data Security

Kathika uses commercially reasonable measures to protect user information, including secure authentication systems, encrypted communication channels, monitoring tools, and access controls.

12. Third-Party Services

Kathika may utilize trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics
  • Razorpay
  • Cloud Messaging Services

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

13. Data Retention

Information may be retained for operational, legal, security, and accounting purposes. Retention periods vary depending on the nature of the data and applicable legal requirements.

14. User Rights

Users may have rights to:

  • Access personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal data
  • Request support related to privacy concerns

15. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion. Certain information may be retained when required by law, security investigations, fraud prevention efforts, or financial compliance obligations.

16. Children's Privacy

Kathika is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

17. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Revised versions will be published on this page with the updated effective date.
