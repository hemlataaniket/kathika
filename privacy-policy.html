# Kathika Privacy Policy Last Updated: June 2026 ## Introduction Welcome to Kathika. Kathika is a digital storytelling platform where users can read stories, write stories, publish content, interact with authors, support creators, subscribe to premium features, and participate in community discussions. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use Kathika. By using Kathika, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. ## Information We Collect ### Account Information When you create an account, we may collect: * Name * Email Address * Username * Profile Photo * Language Preferences ### Profile Information Users may voluntarily provide: * Biography * Social Links * Author Information * Cover Photos * Display Preferences ### Content Information Kathika collects content that users create and publish, including: * Stories * Chapters * Drafts * Comments * Reviews * Posts * Messages * Superfan Interactions ### Transaction Information When users purchase Mohars, subscriptions, or support authors, transaction-related information may be collected through authorized payment providers. Kathika does not store complete payment card details on its own servers. ### Device Information We may collect: * Device Type * Operating System Version * App Version * Crash Reports * Performance Data ### Analytics Information To improve the platform, we may collect: * Reading Activity * Search Activity * Feature Usage Statistics * Session Information * Engagement Metrics ## How We Use Information We use information to: * Create and manage accounts * Provide story reading and writing services * Improve user experience * Deliver personalized recommendations * Process payments and subscriptions * Detect fraud and abuse * Enforce community guidelines * Maintain platform security * Provide customer support ## Story Ownership and Intellectual Property Authors retain ownership of the original content they create and publish on Kathika.
