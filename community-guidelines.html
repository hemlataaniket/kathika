Community Guidelines

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Our Community

Kathika is a platform built for readers, writers, creators, and literature lovers. Our goal is to maintain a respectful, safe, and inspiring environment where creativity can flourish.

2. Respect Other Users

Treat all members with respect and dignity. Healthy discussions are encouraged, but personal attacks, insults, threats, or harassment are not allowed.

3. No Hate Speech

Content that promotes hatred, discrimination, violence, or hostility against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, disability, gender, or other protected characteristics is prohibited.

4. No Harassment or Bullying

Do not target, intimidate, threaten, stalk, or repeatedly harass other users. Such behavior may result in immediate moderation action.

5. Original Content Only

Authors should publish original work or content they have permission to use. Copyright infringement, plagiarism, or unauthorized reproduction of content is not permitted.

6. No Illegal Content

Content that violates laws or promotes illegal activities is prohibited and may be reported to appropriate authorities when required.

7. No Spam

Users may not post repetitive, misleading, promotional, or spam content intended solely to manipulate visibility or engagement.

8. Responsible Story Publishing

Writers are encouraged to publish high-quality content. Misleading titles, deceptive descriptions, fake claims, or intentionally harmful content should be avoided.

9. Comments and Reviews

Comments and reviews should remain constructive and relevant. Abuse, trolling, or attempts to provoke conflict are not acceptable.

10. Profile Conduct

Usernames, profile photos, biographies, and cover images must comply with platform rules and should not contain offensive, misleading, or harmful material.

11. Chat and Communication

Messages and conversations should remain respectful. Unwanted solicitation, harassment, scams, and abusive behavior are prohibited.

12. Superfan Features

Superfan memberships are intended to strengthen creator-reader relationships. Users must not misuse Superfan features for fraud, manipulation, or harassment.

13. Reporting Violations

Users can report stories, comments, messages, posts, or profiles that violate these guidelines. Reports are reviewed by moderation systems and administrators.

14. Moderator Actions

Kathika moderators may take actions including:

  • Content removal
  • Content restriction
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Permanent account bans
  • Feature restrictions

15. Appeals

Users may contact support to request a review of moderation decisions. Appeals will be reviewed based on available evidence and platform policies.

16. Community Responsibility

Every member of Kathika contributes to the health of the community. By following these guidelines, users help create a positive environment for readers and writers alike.

17. Changes to Guidelines

Kathika may update these Community Guidelines from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page.
